Return Policy & Warranty

RETURN POLICY

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you are not satisfied with your purchase you can return your product for a refund. We have a 30-day return policy from ship date. Product must be returned in original packaging and in new condition. Buyer is required to pay all return shipping costs and register their return package with a tracking number. Your receipt is required with your return as well as a Return Merchandise Authorization Number (RMA #). You will be credited for the cost of the item(s) and any applicable tax minus actual shipping costs. Shipping will not be refunded. For returns, exchanges, and defects contact customer service for a RMA # through live chat or at cs@phonesoap.com.

Return packages must include:

  • Receipt of purchase
  • Original packaging
  • Assigned RMA #
  • Tracking

Your refund will be processed once we have safely received the product in our warehouse and you will be notified at that point.

EXCHANGES

You may exchange your item(s) within 30 days of your ship date. Contact customer service through live chat or at cs@phonesoap.com to initiate an exchange. We can immediately ship your new order before we receive your exchange. In this case your card will be charged for the price of the products we shipped and will be credited back once we safely receive your exchange.

Return packages must include:

  • Receipt of purchase
  • Original packaging
  • Assigned RMA #
  • Tracking
